# Kaizen

A Japanese philosophy of continuous improvement through small, incremental changes. Originally applied to business and manufacturing, it is equally applicable to personal life.

## Core Principles

- **Continuous improvement** — progress consistently, even in small steps; do not seek immediate perfection
- **Eliminate waste** — identify habits, tasks, or energy uses that add no value
- **Focus on processes** — build systems and habits rather than fixating on final outcomes
- **Active participation** — each person commits to the change process
- **Regular self-assessment** — reflect on progress and adjust the plan as needed
