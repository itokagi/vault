# Web Design

The practice of designing and building websites — increasingly understood as a discipline of psychology, strategy, and business outcomes rather than visual aesthetics alone.

## The Five Shifts (2025)

1. **[[zero-click-search|Zero-Click Search]]** — AI overviews are absorbing traffic before users ever reach a site; websites must be worth visiting, not just answering questions
2. **[[human-made-design|Human-Made Design]]** — reaction against AI-generated sites; brands differentiate through personality, texture, and real human craft
3. **Psychology-driven design** — [[halo-effect|Halo Effect]], [[cognitive-fluency|Cognitive Fluency]], and [[peak-end-rule|Peak-End Rule]] now drive every design decision
4. **[[mobile-first-design|Mobile-First Design]]** — 60% of web traffic is mobile; mobile must be the primary experience, not an afterthought
5. **[[web-design-as-strategy|Web Design as Strategy]]** — designers must sell outcomes (leads, credibility, trust), not pages

## Key Principle

> The barrier to entry for design has never been lower, but the barrier to doing it *well* has never been higher. That gap is the opportunity.
