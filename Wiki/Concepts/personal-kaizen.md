# Personal Kaizen

The application of the Kaizen philosophy to individual life — a structured strategy for fostering gradual, meaningful changes in productivity, well-being, and emotional balance.

## Key Ideas

- Treats personal development the same way organizations treat process improvement
- Encourages identifying areas for improvement and taking small, continuous actions that compound over time
- Not about dramatic life overhauls — about progress-oriented mindset where every step builds toward lasting transformation

## Adopting the Mindset

- Celebrate small wins as genuine progress
- Build resilience — significant changes require consistency over time
- Establish a clear personal mission aligned with core values
- Manage energy wisely across physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual dimensions

## Goal Setting

- Break large goals into smaller, measurable steps (e.g. "walk 15 minutes a day" not "improve health")
- Review and realign goals periodically as circumstances change
- Balance effort and recovery — life as sprints, not a marathon
