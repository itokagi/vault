# Zero-Click Search

A search that ends on the results page without the user clicking through to any website — increasingly common as AI-generated overviews answer questions directly.

## The Data

> [!warning] Search traffic is collapsing
> - ~60% of all Google searches now end without a single click to a website (SEMrush, 2025)
> - Organic click-through rates have dropped **61%** for queries where AI overviews appear

## What It Means for Websites

- If a website's primary job is to explain things, that job is being automated away
- Google reads content, summarises it, and hands the answer to the user without a visit
- Websites built around "who we are, what we do, how to contact us" are becoming obsolete

## The Fix

Every website must answer: *"If AI could answer every question on my page, would anyone still need to visit?"*

If the answer is no, the site needs to offer something AI cannot replicate:
- A tool or interactive experience
- A community
- A personality or brand voice that is distinctly human
